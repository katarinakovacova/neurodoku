Why Did I Create My Sudoku Project?

This project started as a way for me to learn programming. I wanted a simple, meaningful goal—and Sudoku felt like the perfect fit.

But it’s also deeply personal. Dementia has affected someone close in my family, and I’ve seen how important mental stimulation can be.

I’ve been solving Sudoku puzzles for many years. It was my mom who first introduced me to them, and it quickly became a part of my life.

By creating this app, I wanted to combine learning with purpose—offering something helpful to others while honoring a connection that means a lot to me.
